About​ us

The InfrafrontierGR/Phenotypos database (DB) was created in the context of InfrafrontierGR, the Greek node of INFRAFRONTIER, an open access infrastructure for disease-oriented analyses of precision mutations. 

The DB supports mostly mouse models, however it is developed in a way that makes it an easily extendable and scalable platform for the generation and dissemination of other models and protocols.

The Infrastructure specializes in secondary and tertiary phenotyping in the following disease areas: Inflammatory Bowel Disease, Arthritis, Infection, Neurodegeneration, Mental Disability, Metabolic Disorders and Cancer.

The current database architecture is built to support all phenotyping protocols (SOPS) designed by BSRC Alexander Fleming researchers and Phenotyping Unit members.

The DB was developed to support the storage of phenotyping protocols (SOPs) and the data generated by the associated Units. We are currently working on user-customised data reporting.

To date we have developed more than 40 SOPs on monitoring procedures/assays in the areas of microCT imaging, ultrasound Imaging, optical/X-ray imaging, endoscopy imaging, FACS, Histopathology, Biochemical, Hematology and Clinical Analyses, and Proteomics.
